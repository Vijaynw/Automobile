Lamborghini Huracan LP 580-2 Spyder

The 2016 Lamborghini Huracan LP 580-2 Spyder was created to combine the lifestyle appeal of the convertible with the pure on-road experience of the Huracan's rear-wheel drive technology. The model features a newly designed front and rear ends with a dynamic but more aggressive look to distinguish it from the all-wheel drive version. With the roof down, two distinctive fins rise out of the folding roof-casing to define the unique lines of the car. With the roof up or down, the model promises to offer the same performance and thrills as the coupe variant.
